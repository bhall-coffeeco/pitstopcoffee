Good to know

Common questions.

Still unsure whether the cart fits your event? Send an inquiry and tell us what you are planning.

What types of events do you serve?+

We are building service options for weddings, workplaces, church events, showers, parties, markets, and other gatherings around Springfield.

Can the cart operate indoors?+

Many coffee-cart setups can work indoors or outdoors depending on venue access, available power, weather, and local requirements. We will confirm the setup during planning.

How far in advance should I inquire?+

Earlier is better, especially for weekends and larger events. Sending an inquiry does not obligate you to book.

Can the menu be customized?+

Event menus can be adjusted based on service time, guest count, season, and drink preferences. Include any special requests in your inquiry.

How much does coffee cart catering cost?+

Pricing depends on guest count, service length, location, menu, and setup needs. Share the event details and we will build a custom quote.
